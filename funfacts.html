FunFacts

The Old English word for strawberry is streawberige, and in that language 'strew' and 'straw' were both used to mean 'to spread or scatter'.

#1 No other fruit has seeds on the outside.

#2 They can go in a bouquet.

#3 Strawberries are a member of the rose family!
